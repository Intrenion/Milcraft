**Military thinking is a craft.** Milcraft transforms influential books, doctrine, and military publications into structured practices that capture their essential ideas. Rather than reading summaries, you explore actionable practices, connect ideas across authors, and discuss them with AI. The goal is to help military professionals develop sound judgment through the study of enduring military thought. Milcraft is an independent educational project for anyone committed to the profession of arms. **Contact us:** [Email](mailto:christian.ullrich@intrenion.com) • [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/christianullrich/)
