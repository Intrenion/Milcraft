Military thinking is a craft.

It is developed through the study of enduring ideas, practical experience, and continuous reflection.

Milcraft transforms influential books, doctrine, and military publications into structured practices. Rather than summarizing what an author wrote, each publication is distilled into actionable practices that can be studied, connected, and discussed with AI.

The goal is simple: help military professionals develop sound judgment by making decades of military thought more accessible and easier to apply.

Milcraft is an independent educational project for anyone committed to the profession of arms.
